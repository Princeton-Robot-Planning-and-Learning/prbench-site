Benchmark / Geometric 3D / Shelf3D

Shelf3D

Description

A 3D environment where the goal is to pick up objects from the ground and place them onto a shelf.

Variants

This environment has 5 variant(s).

Shelf3D-o1

This variant has 1 object to place on the shelf.

Shelf3D-o10

This variant has 10 objects to place on the shelf.

Shelf3D-o2

This variant has 2 objects to place on the shelf.

Shelf3D-o3

This variant has 3 objects to place on the shelf.

Shelf3D-o5

This variant has 5 objects to place on the shelf.

References

This is a very common kind of environment. The background is adapted from the Replica dataset (Straub et al., 2019).
