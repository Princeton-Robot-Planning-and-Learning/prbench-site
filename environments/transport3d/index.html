Transport3D

Description

A 3D environment where the goal is to place all objects, including one or more solid cubes and a box, on a table.

Variants

This environment has 2 variant(s).

Transport3D-o1

This variant has 1 cube and 1 box to transport onto the table.

Transport3D-o2

This variant has 2 cubes and 1 box to transport onto the table.
